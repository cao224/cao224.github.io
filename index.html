About me

Hi, my name is Carolyn O'Connor and I am currently a sophmore at Lehigh University pursuing a dual degree in Biology and Computer Science. I grew up in a small town in the far suburbs of Philadelphia, PA with an older brother, a twin sister, and a younger sister. Ever since my brother received a make-your-own radio circuit board kit for Christmas in 2011, the curiousity of the inner workings of electronics progressed until highschool when I encountered my first programming class and instantly peaked my interest in problem-solving and creativity involved in coding. Biology sparked my intrest when a sports injury led to a minimally invasive knee surgery. Since then I have immersed myself in both the programming community and biology community through extracurricular clubs/activities, classes, and volunteer work.
